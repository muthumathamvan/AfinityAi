Setup Instructions

  1. Go to your HubSpot account
  2. Navigate to Settings → Integrations → Private Apps
  3. Create a new private app or use existing one
  4. Grant necessary scopes: contacts (read/write), notes (write)
  5. Copy the access token and portal ID
  6. Paste them in the form and save
